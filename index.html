View your own maps

You can view your own firelines, boundaries and other map features on MAFI by uploading them as a kml or kmz file from from your computer or device.
(These are the files used from Google Earth )

You can then share these with other users using the web address that will be provided after you upload the file.

Only suitable for map features such as simple lines, points or boundaries
